Description

  • Meat: Sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic: 1 onion and 2 cloves of garlic
  • Tomato products: 1 can crushed tomatoes, 2 cans tomato sauce, 2 cans tomato paste
  • Sugar: 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles: Store-bought or homemade
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg: 1 egg (to bind the ricotta mixture)
  1. Make the meat sauce by cooking sausage, beef, onion, and garlic; add tomato products, sugar, and seasonings. Simmer.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare the ricotta mixture by combining ricotta cheese with egg and parsley.
  4. Layer the lasagna in a baking dish (see layering instructions below).
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes.
  6. Uncover and bake for an additional 25 minutes until bubbling and golden.
  7. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving.
  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat layers and finish with remaining Parmesan on top
